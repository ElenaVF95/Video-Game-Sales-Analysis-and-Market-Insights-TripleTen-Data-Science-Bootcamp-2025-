# Project Overview

This project analyzes historical video game sales data to identify market trends, high-performing platforms, regional sales patterns, and factors associated with commercial success. The goal was to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights that could support marketing campaign planning and strategic decision-making.

The analysis included data cleaning, preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), data visualization, and statistical hypothesis testing. Sales performance was evaluated across platforms, genres, regions, and user/critic ratings to better understand which products and markets showed the strongest potential.

# Objective

To identify patterns in video game sales and generate data-driven recommendations for planning future advertising campaigns.

- Main Tasks
- Cleaned and prepared a historical video game sales dataset.
- Analyzed sales trends by platform, genre, year, and region.
- Compared global and regional sales performance.
- Explored the relationship between user/critic scores and sales.
- Created visualizations to communicate key findings.
- Formulated and tested statistical hypotheses.
- Generated business-oriented recommendations based on the analysis.

# Key Outcome

The project delivered actionable insights to identify platforms, genres, and regional markets with stronger growth potential, simulating a real-world business scenario where data analysis supports marketing and strategic planning decisions.
